Topic: At universities and colleges, sports and social activities are just as important as classes and libraries and should receive equal financial support.

Nowadays, as the standard of job applicants getting higher, undergraduate students have no choice but to become more well-rounded developed, that is to say, they should not only acquire a high grade in academic, but also be adept at one or more kinds of sports, or have an excellent social skill. In my point of view, as for universities and colleges, undoubtedly they should provide students with better facilities and financial support for sports and social activities as equal as that for classes and libraries.

Take my university for example, I study in a top university which focuses on electric and electronic engineering, most of my classmates are good at doing research or tackling various engineering problems, however, when it comes to playing sports, most of them would commit they have almost never do any exercise on campus. There are mainly three reasons: first of all, as a science student, we cannot keep up with the classes if we don’t spend most of our time study and do experiments, which, what’s more, is also energy consuming. After a all day study, many of us are tired of moving our bodies anymore. Secondly, the sports facilities in our school are not in a good condition, which holds us back from doing sports. We often find various issues in the sports center, maybe the playground is dusty, or the facilities are worn out, or else the swimming pool is closed due to some reasons. It is really upsetting when we can’t work out in a good environment. Finally, as time changes, the way people entertain changes, too. Obviously, there are more options such as playing computer games, chatting online, watching funny videos on YouTube-Thus, if it is not required, few of us would choose sports as a way to entertain.

As for social activities, students in our school are known for being low in EQ.From my own perspective, the critical factor is that our classes arrangement attaches more importance on science, without including some art classes. Being absorbed in equations and codes, quite a few students lost some crucial skills of social activities, which, sometimes, decide success or loss. Imagin if you have an excellent project and looking for investment from venture capital firms, no matter how advanced your technologies are, if you fail to make a wonderful speech to interpret your product, it is not likely to win the investment. Thus, the importance of social skills cannot be addressed more.

Obviously, universities and college can make a difference to this situation by moving more financial support to sports and social activities. Especially at this very moment, when employment pressure is rising. Universities and college should not only provide the financial support but also leading the students to practice. Organizing more activities like sports meeting, dancing party, speech competition and so on, make sure there are various types of activities so that everyone can find their interest. I believe that for the entire society, this would be an investment with an impressive payback.
