Spring Time

2018-03-10
Blog
约 221 字 预计阅读 2 分钟 次阅读

Spring is here again. Time flies, I have spent 3 years in Xi’an, a dry, antiquated and far-away-from-home city in the northwestern part of China.

In the third year of university life, things are very different from they used to be. Alomost everyone has decided or is deciding their next step, and is moving toward that direction. For me, making a decision is the hardest thing to do. Maybe I’m too afraid of making a mistake, not willing to step out of my confort zone. Remember when I was a kid, in the drawing class, Mr.Liao, who is the chairman of our local art orgnaiztion, always told us to be bold, don’t be afraid of making mistakes, cuz you can always use the lines and colors to cover the imperfect parts and achieve perfection. Don’t let the fear of making mistakes limit your imagination!

I always wanted to go abroad, living in a whole different cultural environment, to see the world in various forms. But the first thing I’m concerned about is the cost of living and the tuition. Some people say it’s an investment, and the payback is going to be impressive. Well, I hope so :)

So, I’m busy preparing for the GRE and TOFEL, in the last year in Xi’an, I hope everyone achieve his or her goals.
